The Nile Through Uganda

From Lake Victoria to the South Sudan border

1. Jinja — Source

The Nile begins its journey at Jinja, flowing out of Lake Victoria as the Victoria Nile.

2. Lake Kyoga

The Victoria Nile flows northwest through Lake Kyoga in central Uganda.

3. Murchison Falls

The river squeezes through a narrow 7-meter gorge, creating one of Africa's most powerful waterfalls.

4. Lake Albert

The Victoria Nile empties into Lake Albert on the Uganda-DRC border.

5. Albert Nile

Exiting Lake Albert, the river becomes the Albert Nile, flowing north. The West Nile region lies to its west.

6. Nimule — Exit

The Albert Nile crosses into South Sudan at Nimule, continuing as the White Nile toward Khartoum.

West Nile Region

The orange-shaded area west of the Albert Nile — including Arua, Nebbi, Moyo, and surrounding districts.
